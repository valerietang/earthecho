Eco-Friendly Tips

  • Reduce single-use plastics by using reusable bags and containers.
  • Conserve energy by turning off lights and unplugging devices when not in use.
  • Use public transportation, bike, or walk to reduce your carbon footprint.
  • Support sustainable brands and shop locally.
  • Practice composting to reduce food waste.
